# Japanese Automakers Equity Research 📊

Brief equity research project for **FINA2320 Investments and Portfolio Analysis**, focused on three Japanese automotive companies:

**Toyota Motor Corp (7203 JP)**  
Buy recommendation based on its hybrid leadership, strong profitability, global scale, and multi-pathway transition strategy.

**Suzuki Motor Corp (7269 JP)**  
Strong Buy recommendation driven by its dominant position in India, production expansion, compact SUV exposure, and attractive valuation upside.

**Subaru Corp (7270 JP)**  
Hold recommendation due to its strong balance sheet and North American SUV franchise, balanced against tariff risk, FX exposure, and electrification costs.

## Project Overview

The project compares the companies across business model, geographic exposure, financial performance, industry positioning, valuation, investment thesis, catalysts, and risks.

## Repository Contents

- `investments_toyota.pdf` — Toyota equity research report
- `investments_suzuki.pdf` — Suzuki equity research report
- `investments_subaru.pdf` — Subaru equity research report

## Key Takeaway

Toyota offers the most defensive transition story, Suzuki presents the strongest growth case through India, while Subaru remains financially solid but more exposed to near-term earnings pressure.
